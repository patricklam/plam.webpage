Regexps

Here are some more links for regular expressions (thanks Mehdi):

  • Practical Programming in Tcl and Tk, 3rd Edition, by Brent Welch, 2003. (Chapter 11 is on Regular Expressions and you can access it online.)
  • Regular Expression Pocket Reference, 2nd Edition, by Tony Stubblebine, O`Reilly Media Inc., 2007. (You can access it online at Safari.)
  • Regular Expressions quick reference sheets: sheet 1 or sheet 2.

You can explore regular expressions using the following tool: http://gskinner.com/RegExr/. Thanks to Aly Hirani for the link!

Another useful reference is at http://www.regular-expressions.info, which says much more about regular expressions in practice than I possibly could in lecture.
