Software Engineering Work Term Report To Do List

author: Jon Shahen

  • Open Software Engineering Work Term Report To Do List
  • First page of report must include a TITLE followed by your NAME
  • Below your NAME you must include a list of 3 COURSES that most closely resemble the work discussed in this report
  • (Optional) Indicate below COURSES, you may indicate if you want to be graded by recent Software Engineering alumni
  • Font size of the body should be easily readable for the marker
  • PDF is the only acceptable format
  • Report length should be between 2000 and 3000 words long
  • Your work report must describe a design decision (or a series of related smaller decisions) and justify a choice
  • The report can be in an engineering format or a blog-style post
  • Work reports must be free of spelling mistakes. (Spell checkers exist; use them!)
  • Reports are due 7 calendar days after the first day of lectures; late submissions will be given a failing grade and graded the following term
